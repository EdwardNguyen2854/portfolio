## Introduction
Building a portfolio shouldn't be complicated. In this post, I'll share how I used Jekyll to create a maintainable, data-driven site.

## Why Jekyll?
- **Static Site Generation**: Fast, secure, and free hosting on GitHub Pages.
- **Data Support**: `_data` folders make managing content easy.
- **Customizability**: Full control over HTML and CSS.
